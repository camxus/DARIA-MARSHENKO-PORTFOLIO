/ohne unabhängigkeit/ & /ohne freiheit/ 2021 
after reading richard sennett’s "flesh and stone" i started thinking about the permanent interdependence between people and everything they build/create around themselves. and like everything in life it has both positive and negative sides. i wanted to focus on the reverse effect of achieving something: the more we strive- the greater the level of our responsibility is- therefore the more captivity it entails. i wanted to reflect this unpleasant feeling of coming to terms with self-imprisonment
